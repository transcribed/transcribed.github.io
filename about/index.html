About

Serving those who want sermons in writing.

There are so many reasons why we need the written words of our sermons online. The Deaf deserve more access to the gospel. The words of our church leaders should show up in Google search results. You shouldn't have to waste time fast-forwarding and rewinding the podcast to find that great quote. And you shouldn't stress about taking perfect notes each Sunday. (Although if you take notes to stay awake, keep writing!)

I could use your help! The first way you can help is to share this website via social media. But I could also use help transcribing. Hit me up at transcribedsermons [at] gmail [dot] com to get involved.
